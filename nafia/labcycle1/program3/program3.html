About us....

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of technology named after the late PM sri.RAjiv Gandhi, run by the Government of Kerala, started functioning in 1991. It has established a vast infrastructure and put together a team of dedicated teachers. The institution has become one of the leading technical Institutes in Kerala.
